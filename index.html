Puzzle Trainer

An online chess puzzle trainer similar to Puzzle Rush. Solve as many puzzles as you can in 2 minutes and see how you rank! Made with React, Express, and Firebase.

Fast-Slow Debugger

A debugging tool to compare a slow, but correct solution against a fast, but not necessarily correct solution. Made with HTML, CSS, JS, Node, Express, and Python.

A Discord bot made to assist chess players. Analyze your games with Stockfish, find puzzles from your games, and convert a picture of a scoresheet to a pgn file. Made with Python.
